Introduction

PT. Seaweed Anugrah Nusantara Kita (SANK) is an Indonesia-based company engaged in seaweed cultivation, processing, carbon trading, and educational tourism based on seaweed ecosystems. We are committed to building a sustainable maritime industry that supports both environmental protection and the welfare of coastal communities.

By integrating eco-friendly principles into every stage of our production and business operations, SANK contributes to the advancement of the national blue economy and the preservation of marine ecosystems through our blue carbon initiatives.

Company Vision

To become a global pioneer in sustainable seaweed industry in Indonesia through innovation, environmental sustainability, and coastal economic empowerment based on the blue carbon ecosystem.

Company Mission
  • Develop seaweed cultivation with international standards.
  • Produce high-value derivative seaweed products.
  • Promote the blue carbon trade industry.
  • Build marine conservation-based educational tourism.
  • Empower the economic growth of coastal communities.

Company History

The journey of PT. Seaweed Anugrah Nusantara Kita (SANK) in building a competitive and sustainable Indonesian seaweed industry.

The Beginning

PT. SANK was established in 2025 by a group of seaweed experts, environmental specialists, and social entrepreneurs sharing a common vision to develop a sustainable maritime ecosystem.

Organic Cultivation

Starting from an organic seaweed cultivation project with coastal communities, SANK has evolved into a sustainable business model based on the blue economy.

Development & Expansion

Expanding its operations into the processing industry and becoming a pioneer of blue carbon trading in Indonesia with the support of scientific research.

Today, PT. SANK has grown into an integrated maritime company committed to sustainability, technological innovation, and the empowerment of Indonesia’s coastal communities.

Core Business

SANK builds an integrated seaweed industry ecosystem — from cultivation and production to the global market — guided by principles of sustainability and the blue economy.

Seaweed Cultivation

Organic cultivation projects across Sumenep, Sulawesi, Bali, NTB, and NTT featuring premium varieties such as Eucheuma cottonii, Sargassum, and Gracilaria.

  • Chemical-free & eco-friendly methods
  • Rotational farming to protect marine ecosystems
  • Supports blue carbon storage initiatives
Modern Processing

Green technology-based production delivering high-quality, export-grade organic products.

  • Agar, natural cosmetics & organic fertilizers
  • Certified Halal, Organic & ISO 14001
  • Energy-efficient systems powered by solar energy
Educational Eco-Tourism

Marine-based educational tourism focusing on conservation and community empowerment.

  • Eco-tours & seaweed farm visits
  • Workshops on processing & environmental awareness
  • Empowerment programs for coastal MSMEs
